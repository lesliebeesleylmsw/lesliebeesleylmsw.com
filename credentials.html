Credentials

I am a Licensed Master Social Worker in the state of Kansas. I received my graduate degree from the University of Oklahoma (OU). During my time at OU I completed my internship providing individual and group therapy at the Dr. Eddie Warrior Correctional Center. Post graduate experience includes providing individual and family therapy in community mental health, primarily treating trauma, grief, and issues related to parenting. Additional professional experience includes hospice, supervised visitation, and providing therapy and facilitating parenting groups in the child welfare setting.
