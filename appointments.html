Appointments

Convenient

I provide a safe, confidential space, and I offer convenient appointment times including early mornings, during the lunch hour, evenings, and Saturdays.

Affordable

The ability for all to afford mental healthcare is important to me. As such I will honor your insurance copay fee as I am not currently set up to accept insurance.

For those without insurance I offer a sliding scale fee in an effort to make mental health services affordable for all.
